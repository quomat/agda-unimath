## Idea

A square of [homotopies](foundation-core.homotopies.md)

```text
          top
      f ------> g
      |         |
 left |         | right
      v         v
      h ------> i
        bottom
```

is said to **commute** if there is a homotopy `left ∙h bottom ~ top ∙h right `.
Such a homotopy is called a **coherence** of the square.
