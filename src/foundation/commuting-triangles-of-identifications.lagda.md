## Idea

A triangle of [identifications](foundation-core.identity-types.md)

```text
 x ----- y
  \     /
   \   /
    \ /
     z
```

is said to **commute** if there is a higher identification between the `x ＝ z`
and the concatenated identification `x ＝ y ＝ z`.
